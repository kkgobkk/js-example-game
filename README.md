# js-example-game

An example game made with vanilla js only. Run by opening "index.html" in any browser (preferably chromium or firefox based, and in a fullscreen window)

## controls
* use WASD to move
* left-click to shoot
* right-click for a slower attack with increased damage

## enemies
* **normal**: square enemies that take 1 hit
* **runners**: tall enemies who move faster
* **tanks**: large enemies take multiple hits to kill
* **rangers**: small enemies shoot projectiles at you
* **jugglers**: enemies who summon rotating projectiles around them

## power-ups
* the orange power-up gives you a few points
* the green power-up heals you (and give you shields if your HP is full)
* the blue power-up gives you temporary invincibility
* the purple power-up is a surprise
